About Us

At orogram, we use escrow payment for all transactions, both public and private, which ensures that your assets are always safe. Whether you're buying or selling products or services, you can rest assured that your funds are protected.

We take security seriously and have implemented strict measures to prevent illegal activities from taking place on our platform. All transactions are processed through smart contracts, which helps to prevent money laundering and suspicious activities. Additionally, our platform is regularly monitored to ensure that all transactions are carried out in a transparent and secure manner.

We understand the importance of having low transaction fees, which is why we charge only 1% for P2P transactions and 2% for buying and selling products and services. There are no transaction fees for sending and receiving orogram coin, making it one of the most affordable stable coins available.

We believe in equality and fairness, which is why we do not store or sell any kind of cookies and we do not share them with any companies. All users are treated the same on our platform and we do not have any control over manipulating or making differences between users.

Our platform is user-friendly and easy to use, with a simple and straightforward interface. We do not store any private keys of our users, which ensures that your assets are always under your control.

We take responsibility for ensuring that orogram coin follows the price of 24 carat gold, and we charge 1% on top of the gold price if you buy orogram, and 1% less than the price of gold if you sell orogram to us.

In conclusion, orogram is the stable coin that offers security, stability, and affordability. With its 24 carat gold backing, escrow payment, and low transaction fees, orogram is the perfect platform for buying and selling products and services. Join us today and experience the future of stable coin.
